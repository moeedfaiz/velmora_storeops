# Velmora Returns & Exchanges Policy (Excerpt)

- Items can be returned within 30 days if *unworn*, *unwashed*, and with tags attached.
- Worn or damaged items are **not eligible** unless the item was defective at delivery.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after inspection.
- Exchange requests must specify size/color and are subject to stock availability.
- Orders marked "Shipped" can be canceled only via support within 1 hour of status change.
